On the day

Here are a few things important for the day:

  • Make sure the room has drinks and snacks. If you are going to have a room it's great to ensure that has water and snacks for your contributor. They are going to be working hard so lets reward them!
  • If you plan to use slides check the venue can use them.
  • Try and get a room with a board to write things like useful urls out.
  • Take the names of the people attending as we as a team would love to know and follow up with them after. It's useful to get the wordpress.org username and full name.
