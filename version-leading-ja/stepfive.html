Theme Review

After the event.

Once the event is over there are a few things that it's great to do:

  • Do a report back to the theme review team. Let us know how the event went!
  • Let us know how this packed worked. Is there anything we should add?
  • Check all themes have been re-allocated if you had to assign to yourself.
  • Make sure you notify the team of the mentor and mentee assignments for themes.
  • Pat yourself on the back. Every theme review counts, so thank you.
  • Don't stop. Check in on the reviewers. How are they doing? Do you want help form the team to mentor them and keep things going? Let us know!
  • Run another session. It doesn't matter if a day in a classroom or a few hours in a coffee shop; every review matters.
