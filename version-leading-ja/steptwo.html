Before the event

Here are a few things we'd recommend before your event to make sure things run smoothly.

  • Promotion. Let people know about the event. Shout out in the weekly chat or in Slack on #themereview and make sure we announce the event on our make.blog.
  • Checking connections. If you are organising it or can contact the person who is, find out if you will have internet or not. This will shape some of what you can do.
  • Have fun. This is an awesome thing you are doing so enjoy it!
