Theme Review

Communication

The main form of communication is through our blog and also through Slack. You can join the slack channel #themereview - to find out more about Slack visit here. This is the best way to contact the team.

Meetings

We also have 2 meetings a week in #themereview:

  • Tuesday 18:00 UTC team chat.
  • Thursday 18:00 UTC mentoring chat.

Get access to trac

Get access to trac. All the themes live at themes.trac.wordpress.org. You will need access to this with a WordPress.org user account.
